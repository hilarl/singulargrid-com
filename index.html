GRID

Grid lets you connect your Nodes with a global network of connected ecosystems and exchange information securely.

Nodes

Nodes provide encrypted and secure storage for your data. Information stored in Nodes are stored in a decentralized storage system.

Bridge

An adaptor that creates relationships between nodes. The bridge enables Nodes to exchange data and access services that are part of the Grid.

IDENTITY

Identity gives you access to all services within the SingularGrid. It makes it supereasy to manage access to your personal information across all your devices. It saves time by turning the authentication process into just one step.

Identity

You own and control your identity. Your personal information is stored on your device itself. All your data is encrypted and stored in the SingularGrid's decentralized network, only your device can unlock them.

Key

The Key enables contacless sharing of your data without having to worry about privacy, you choose what data to share, and with whom.

APPS

SingularGrid lets you access global network of connected apps and services.

CLIMATE

SingularGrid's powerful blockchain technology unlocks whole new possibilities for taking climate action.
